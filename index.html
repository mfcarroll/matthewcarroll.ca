I build digital tools and manage technology and security at Stand.earth, an environmental advocacy organization working across Canada and the US.

Before that: twenty-odd years in the nonprofit, public, and private sectors as a scientist, campaigner, and developer — co-founding Leadnow.ca, directing campaigns at openmedia.ca building training software for the Canadian Forces, and more. Selected public projects

  • extract-otp-web

    A client-side tool for extracting one-time-password secrets from Google Authenticator exports. Runs entirely in your browser — nothing leaves your machine.

  • vcard-qr

    A QR-code based contact-card generator and related tools built for Stand.earth staff, with Apple Wallet and Google Wallet integrations for digital business cards — including Apple Passkit certificate-signing backend on Cloud Run.

  • bc-mla-tools

    Tools for working with BC Legislature and MLA data: a scraper that publishes fresh MLA contact data nightly, with an API, Python module, and lightweight embeddable widget that looks up a voter's electoral district from their address.
